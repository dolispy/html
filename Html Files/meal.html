Origin of Pancakes

Most people think Pancakes originated from the United States, interestingly, that is not the case as the earliest mention of Pancakes came from The Ancient Greeks which were called τηγανίτης (tēganitēs), ταγηνίτης (tagēnitēs) or ταγηνίας (tagēnias),all words deriving from τάγηνον (tagēnon), "frying pan". Tagenites were made with wheat flour, olive oil, honey, and curdled milk, and were served for breakfast. So, what is a Pancake?
A pancake (or hot-cake, griddlecake, or flapjack) is a flat cake, often thin and round, prepared from a starch-based batter that may contain eggs, milk and butter and cooked on a hot surface such as a griddle or frying pan, often frying with oil or butter. It is a type of batter bread.

Ingredients for Fluffy Pancakes

  1. 1 Cup of Flour
  2. 1 Cup of Milk
  3. 1 Egg (separate egg white from yolk & whip the egg white only till it's stiff)
  4. 2 Tablespoon of Melted Butter or Vegetable oil
  5. 2 Tablespoon of Sugar
  6. 2 Tablespoon of Baking Powder
  7. ½ Tablespoon of Vanilla Extract (Optional)
  8. A pinch of Salt

Steps to Prepare this

  • Mix all the dry ingredients together - flour, baking powder, salt and sugar
  • Add the separated egg yolk,melted butter , milk & vanilla extract
  • Mix both into a batter
  • Add the egg white and mix
  • Pre-heat a frying pan (preferably, a non-stick pan) and grease the pan with cooking oil or butter
  • Pour the batter into the pan using ¼ cup (approximately) for each pancake, cook until the bubbles break on the surface, and edges just begin to dry.
  • Flip once, and cook until golden brown from both sides
  • Serve Hot.
    • You can serve with Maple Syrup(recommended), Butter, Fruits, or eat plain!
